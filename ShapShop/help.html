Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse products, add them to your cart, and checkout using your preferred payment method.

How do I track my order?

Go to your Profile and click on "Order Status" to see updates.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes! Go to Manage Address in your profile and update your info.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal.

How do I contact support?

You can email us at support@shapshop.com or use the contact form below.
